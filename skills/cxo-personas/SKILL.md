# CXO Personas Library Skill


## 2. Core Rules

### Rule 1: Executive-Only
CXO Personas apply **only** to C-suite and VP-level attendees. For Manager/IC-level attendees, do NOT use personas — prepare based on their specific role and context.

### Rule 2: Confirm Before Applying
Always confirm the target persona using the Title Mapping Guide or Topic Routing Guide before loading a persona file. If a title doesn't map clearly, ask the sales rep.

### Rule 3: Load Full Profile
The INDEX provides summaries only. Always load the **full persona file** for each executive attendee to get complete guidance on priorities, pain points, communication style, objection handling, and buying dynamics.

---

## 3. How to Use

### Step 1: Identify the Persona
Use the **Title Mapping Guide** in [references/INDEX.md](references/INDEX.md) to match job titles to persona files.

### Step 2: Load the Persona
Read the full persona file for each matched executive.

### Step 3: Apply Across Documents
Pull relevant insights and apply them:

| Document | How to Apply Persona |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Call Plan** | Shape discovery questions, objection responses, and agenda tone |
| **Executive Briefing** | Weave into Attendee Background dimensions — do not add a separate persona summary |
| **Engagement Plan** | Inform the "What They Care About" and "How to Win Them" dimensions in People & Objectives |

### Step 4: Multi-Persona Deals
When multiple executives are involved:
- Load each relevant persona
- Tailor messaging per executive
- Reference the **Cross-Persona Dynamics** section in INDEX.md for buying committee patterns

## 6. Each Persona File Contains

Every persona file follows a standardized template covering:
- **Role Definition** — What the executive owns and how they're measured
- **Strategic Priorities** — Their top agenda items
- **KPIs & Success Metrics** — How they define success
- **Pain Points / Challenges** — Core business + AI-specific challenges
- **AI Opportunities** — Where AI creates value for this role
- **Desired Outcomes** — What "good" looks like to them
- **Technology Evaluation Style** — How they assess vendors and solutions
- **Buying Dynamics** — Their role in purchasing decisions
- **Discovery Questions** — Questions to uncover their needs
- **Relationship Map** — Key internal stakeholders they work with
- **Do's & Don'ts** — Quick reference for engagement

---

## 7. Language & Tone

When presenting persona insights to sales reps:
- Be practical and actionable — "Ask this question" not "Consider exploring"
- Use the persona's own vocabulary when coaching
- Highlight what to avoid as clearly as what to do

### Bilingual Support
- Chinese input → Chinese output; English input → English output
- Persona names and titles stay in English
